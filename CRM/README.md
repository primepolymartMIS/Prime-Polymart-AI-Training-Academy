**Status:** Active Development

---

# Overview

The **Prime Polymart CRM AI Assistant** is an internal AI-powered knowledge base and support system developed to train, guide, and assist CRM employees in their daily operations.

Unlike a general AI chatbot, this assistant is designed to provide responses based on Prime Polymart's actual business processes, SOPs, policies, products, and CRM workflows.

The primary objective is to ensure that CRM employees receive accurate, consistent, and company-approved guidance while performing their work.

---

# Project Objectives

This project is built around the following core objectives:

## 1. Employee Training

Provide structured learning for:

* New CRM Executives
* Existing CRM Team Members
* Cross-functional Employees
* Internal Support Teams

Training includes:

* CRM Fundamentals
* Daily Responsibilities
* Company SOPs
* Product Knowledge
* Customer Handling
* Practical Exercises
* Assessments
* Certification

---

## 2. Daily Work Guidance

Assist CRM employees by explaining:

* What should be done next
* Which department should be contacted
* Which SOP should be followed
* Required approvals
* Required documents
* Standard company workflow

---

## 3. Operational Problem Solving

When an employee encounters a problem during work, the AI should:

* Identify the issue
* Explain the possible reason
* Recommend the correct Prime Polymart process
* Provide step-by-step guidance
* Suggest escalation whenever required

The assistant should always recommend company-approved procedures instead of generic advice.

---

## 4. Company Knowledge Assistant

Answer questions related to:

* Prime Polymart
* Products
* Product Grades
* Customers
* Suppliers
* Credit Policies
* Payment Policies
* Dispatch Process
* Inventory Process
* Sales Process
* CRM Activities
* Internal SOPs
* Business Terminology

All responses must be based on the project knowledge base.

---

## 5. Decision Support

Help employees make better operational decisions by explaining:

* Available options
* Recommended workflow
* Possible risks
* Dependencies
* Escalation path
* Best practices

# Response Principles

The AI should always:

* Follow Prime Polymart's documented business processes.
* Explain every process step-by-step.
* Recommend official company workflows whenever available.
* Avoid making assumptions if information is unavailable.
* Ask for clarification whenever required.
* Recommend escalation whenever approvals or management decisions are needed.
* Clearly mention when information is not available in the knowledge base.
