# Company Overview

Prime Polymart Private Limited is an Indian polymer distribution and import company specializing in engineering plastics, commodity polymers, biodegradable polymers, and specialty plastic raw materials.

The company imports products from multiple countries and supplies a wide range of polymer grades to manufacturers across India.

Prime Polymart focuses on providing reliable products, fast customer support, and efficient order processing through well-defined internal workflows and digital systems.

# Departments

## Sales Department

### Primary Responsibilities

* Generate new business opportunities.
* Develop new customers.
* Understand market strategy.
* Follow up with customers.
* Handle CRR and NBD activities.
* Coordinate with CRM for order execution.

---

## CRM Department

### Primary Responsibilities

* Coordinate with Sales Executives.
* Receive customer orders.
* Create Sales Orders in Tally.
* Fill the AppSheet Sales Order Form.
* Update order records in Google Sheets.
* Share Sales Order confirmations with customers.
* Payment follow-up.
* Credit limit follow-up.
* PDC follow-up.
* Coordinate with internal departments until customer delivery.

---

## Logistics Department

### Primary Responsibilities

* Verify order details.
* Coordinate with transporters.
* Coordinate with vehicle drivers.
* Manage loading and unloading.
* Verify products and quantities.
* Ensure timely dispatch.
* Monitor transportation until delivery.

---

## Accounts Department

### Primary Responsibilities

* Generate invoices.
* Monitor credit limit alerts.
* Maintain financial records.
* Perform accounting activities related to order processing.

---

## MIS Department

### Primary Responsibilities

* Develop and maintain internal software solutions.
* Manage Google Sheets and Excel systems.
* Create and maintain FMS and IMS.
* Build dashboards and reports.
* Automate business processes.
* Maintain business data.
* Support departments through analytics and automation.

---

# CRM Department Overview

The CRM Department acts as the operational bridge between Sales, Customers, Accounts, Logistics, and MIS.

Its primary objective is to ensure that every customer order is processed accurately, communicated properly, tracked efficiently, and completed according to the company's standard operating procedures.
