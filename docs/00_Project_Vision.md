# Prime Polymart AI Training Academy (PPATA)

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Document Type:** Project Vision  
**Status:** Active  

---

# 1. Vision

To build an AI-powered Corporate Training Academy that delivers consistent, role-based, and practical employee training using company knowledge, SOPs, workflows, assessments, and real business scenarios.

The academy should act as a virtual senior trainer who is available 24×7, ensuring every employee receives the same high-quality training before handling live work.

---

# 2. Mission

The mission of PPATA is to:

- Standardize employee onboarding
- Reduce dependency on manual trainers
- Improve employee confidence
- Increase productivity
- Reduce operational mistakes
- Preserve organizational knowledge
- Make training measurable

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Traditional employee training has several challenges:

- Training quality depends on the trainer.
- Important information is sometimes missed.
- SOPs are difficult to remember.
- New employees repeatedly ask the same questions.
- Managers spend significant time on onboarding.
- There is limited visibility into trainee progress.

PPATA addresses these challenges with structured AI-guided learning.

---

# 4. Project Objectives

The academy should enable employees to:

- Understand the company
- Learn department responsibilities
- Follow SOPs accurately
- Use company software correctly
- Handle real-world scenarios
- Pass assessments
- Become work-ready

---

# 5. Success Metrics

The project is considered successful when:

- Every new employee completes training before live work.
- Training quality remains consistent.
- Employees pass the final assessment.
- Managers spend less time on onboarding.
- SOP compliance improves.
- Operational errors decrease.

# 8. AI Principles

The AI Trainer should always:

- Be professional.
- Be patient.
- Encourage learning.
- Never guess company policies.
- Prefer uploaded documents over assumptions.
- Explain concepts step by step.
- Give practical examples.
- Ask follow-up questions.
- Confirm understanding before progressing.

---

# 9. Learning Philosophy

Every employee learns differently.

The academy should adapt by:

- Simplifying explanations
- Providing additional examples
- Offering hints
- Repeating difficult topics
- Increasing question difficulty gradually

# 11. AI Trainer Roles

The academy consists of several logical trainer roles.

## Welcome Coach

Introduces the company.

## Company Trainer

Explains company policies, products, and structure.

## Department Trainer

Teaches department-specific workflows.

## Practical Coach

Provides hands-on exercises.

## Examiner

Conducts quizzes and assessments.

## Evaluator

Reviews answers and provides feedback.

## Certification Manager

Determines training completion.
