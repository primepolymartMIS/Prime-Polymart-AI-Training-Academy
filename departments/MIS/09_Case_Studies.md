# Purpose

This module presents realistic business situations based on daily operations at Prime Polymart Pvt. Ltd. The objective is to help trainees apply company SOPs, analyze business problems, make informed decisions, and strengthen their operational judgment.

Unlike quizzes, there may be multiple valid approaches to a case study. The trainee is expected to justify decisions using company procedures and business logic.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this module, trainees should be able to:

- Analyze business situations logically.
- Apply relevant SOPs.
- Identify root causes of problems.
- Recommend corrective actions.
- Decide when to escalate an issue.
- Explain decisions clearly and professionally.

---

# AI Trainer Instructions

For every case study:

- Present only one case at a time.
- Do not reveal the solution immediately.
- Allow the trainee to analyze the situation.
- Ask follow-up questions when necessary.
- Evaluate reasoning, not only the final answer.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Reference related SOPs wherever applicable.

# Difficulty Levels

## Level 1 – Beginner

Simple operational situations suitable for New Joinees.

Examples:

- Formula not working
- Missing customer record
- Wrong report date
- Incorrect file sharing
- Duplicate entry

---

## Level 2 – Intermediate

Operational decision-making for MIS Executives.

Examples:

- Dashboard mismatch
- Credit limit discrepancy
- Purchase data inconsistency
- Inventory mismatch
- Payment follow-up issue

---

## Level 3 – Advanced

Business decision-making for Senior MIS Executives.

Examples:

- Multiple dashboards showing different values
- Automation failure before management review
- Incorrect KPI presented to directors
- Major data integrity issue
- Cross-department reporting conflict

---

# Standard Case Study Format

Every case study should include:

- Case Number
- Difficulty Level
- Department
- Business Background
- Current Situation
- Available Information
- Problem Statement
- Questions for Trainee
- Expected Learning
- Related SOPs
- Related Training Modules

# AI Evaluation Criteria

Evaluate the trainee based on:

- Problem Understanding
- SOP Compliance
- Business Logic
- Decision Making
- Root Cause Analysis
- Communication
- Escalation Judgment
- Preventive Thinking

---

# AI Feedback Format

After evaluation, provide:

- Overall Score (0–100)
- Strengths
- Improvement Areas
- Correct Business Approach
- Related SOPs
- Recommended Revision
- Similar Practice Case

---

# Escalation Guidelines

The trainee should identify whether the issue can be:

- Solved independently.
- Resolved through SOP verification.
- Escalated to Senior MIS Executive.
- Escalated to Director.
- Escalated to the IT/Automation Team.

The AI Trainer should evaluate whether the chosen escalation path is appropriate.

After completing this module, trainees should be able to:

- Handle real business situations confidently.

- Make structured business decisions.
- Identify risks before they become major issues.
- Support management with reliable recommendations.
