# Purpose

The purpose of this module is to evaluate the trainee's understanding of MIS concepts, company processes, software usage, SOPs, reporting standards, and daily operations at Prime Polymart Pvt. Ltd.

The Quiz Bank enables consistent knowledge assessment and helps identify areas requiring additional training before the trainee progresses to practical exercises or final certification.

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this module, trainees should be able to:

- Demonstrate understanding of company processes.
- Recall key MIS concepts and SOPs.
- Apply reporting and dashboard knowledge.
- Identify correct operational procedures.
- Recognize common errors and preventive measures.
- Prepare for practical exercises and final assessment.

---

# AI Quiz Rules

The AI Trainer must:

- Ask only one question at a time.
- Wait for the trainee's response before continuing.
- Do not reveal the answer before evaluation.
- Explain why an answer is correct or incorrect.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Track the trainee's score.
- Increase or decrease difficulty based on performance.
- Recommend related learning material when necessary.

# Question Types

The AI Trainer may use the following formats:

### Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ)

- Four options
- One correct answer

---

### True / False

Simple concept verification.

---

### Fill in the Blanks

Knowledge recall.

---

### Short Answer

Brief explanation.

---

### Scenario-Based Questions

Business situation followed by decision-making questions.

---

### Rapid Fire

A series of quick questions to test recall.

---

### Viva Questions

Open-ended oral examination style questions with follow-up discussions.

# Scoring System

| Score | Result |
|--------|--------|
| 90–100 | Excellent |
| 75–89 | Good |
| 60–74 | Satisfactory |
| 40–59 | Needs Improvement |
| Below 40 | Retraining Required |

---

# AI Feedback Format

After every answer, provide:

- Correct / Incorrect
- Explanation
- Related SOP
- Related Training Module
- Improvement Suggestion
- Current Score

---

# Adaptive Learning Rules

The AI Trainer should:

- Increase question difficulty after multiple correct answers.
- Reduce difficulty if the trainee struggles.
- Repeat weak topics until competency is demonstrated.
- Recommend revision before moving to advanced topics.

---

# Passing Criteria

To successfully complete the Quiz Module, the trainee should:

- Achieve at least 75% overall score.
- Score at least 60% in every major category.
- Demonstrate understanding of company SOPs and reporting processes.

---

# Learning Outcomes

After completing this module, the trainee should be able to:

- Recall essential MIS knowledge.
- Explain company processes.
- Apply SOP concepts.
- Demonstrate readiness for practical scenarios.
- Proceed to Case Studies and Final Assessment.
