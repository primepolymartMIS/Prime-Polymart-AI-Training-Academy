# Welcome

Welcome to the **MIS Executive Training Program**.

This training program has been designed to prepare employees for the role of an MIS Executive at Prime Polymart Pvt. Ltd.

The program combines company knowledge, business processes, software training, practical exercises, real-world scenarios, and assessments to ensure that every trainee is fully prepared before handling live work.

The training follows a structured learning path and should be completed in the recommended sequence.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this program is to:

- Standardize MIS Executive training.
- Build a strong understanding of company processes.
- Teach employees how to use company tools and systems.
- Develop analytical and reporting skills.
- Improve data accuracy and decision-making.
- Reduce onboarding time.
- Prepare employees for independent work.

---

# Target Audience

This program is designed for:

- New MIS Executives
- Existing MIS Executives requiring refresher training
- Team Leaders
- Department Managers
- Cross-functional employees learning MIS operations

---

# Learning Objectives

After completing this training program, trainees should be able to:

- Understand the responsibilities of an MIS Executive.
- Follow company Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
- Use approved software and reporting tools.
- Prepare and maintain reports accurately.
- Validate and analyze business data.
- Identify and resolve common data issues.
- Communicate effectively with internal departments.
- Complete assigned MIS tasks independently.

---

# Training Structure

The program is divided into structured learning modules.

The recommended sequence is:

Module 1 – Company Orientation

Module 2 – MIS Executive Role

Module 3 – Google Sheets & Excel for Prime Polymart

Module 4 – Company Data Structure

Module 5 – Daily MIS Operations

Module 6 – Reports & Dashboards

Module 7 – Data Validation & Quality

Module 8 – Business Communication

Module 9 – Advanced MIS Tasks

Module 10 – Practical Projects

Module 11 – Final Assessment

Module 12 – Certification

---

# Learning Method

Each lesson follows a consistent process.

1. Introduction
2. Learning Objectives
3. Concept Explanation
4. Company Example
5. Practical Demonstration
6. Practical Exercise
7. Knowledge Check
8. Quiz
9. Feedback
10. Lesson Summary

---

# Assessment

The training program includes:

- Knowledge Checks
- Practical Exercises
- Module Quizzes
- Case Studies
- Final Assessment

The AI Trainer will provide feedback after each assessment and recommend additional learning if required.

---

# Certification

After successfully completing all required modules and assessments, the AI Trainer will recommend one of the following outcomes:

- Ready for Live Work
- Ready with Supervision
- Additional Training Required

Final certification remains subject to company approval.

---

# Expected Behaviour

Trainees are expected to:

- Participate actively.
- Complete all practical exercises.
- Ask questions whenever clarification is required.
- Follow company policies and SOPs.
- Maintain professionalism throughout the training.

# Program Completion

The training program is considered complete when:
