# MIS Executive Learning Path

**Department:** Management Information System (MIS)  
**Document Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Active  
**Owner:** Prime Polymart Pvt. Ltd.

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the complete learning journey for an MIS Executive at Prime Polymart Pvt. Ltd.

The learning path is designed to transform a new employee into a competent MIS Executive through structured learning, practical exercises, assessments, and real business scenarios.

Every trainee should complete the modules in the recommended sequence unless otherwise instructed by management.

---

# 2. Program Overview

The MIS Executive Training Program consists of twelve structured modules.

Each module builds upon the knowledge gained in previous modules.

The AI Trainer should ensure that trainees successfully complete one module before progressing to the next.

# 4. Module Details

## Module 1 – Company Orientation

### Objective

Introduce the trainee to Prime Polymart Pvt. Ltd., its culture, products, departments, policies, and business operations.

### Topics

- Company Overview
- Vision & Mission
- Organization Structure
- Products
- Company Values
- HR Policies
- Workplace Expectations

### Practical Exercise

Identify the purpose of each department and explain how they work together.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee understands the company's structure and policies.

---

## Module 2 – MIS Executive Role

### Objective

Understand the responsibilities, expectations, and contribution of an MIS Executive.

### Topics

- Roles & Responsibilities
- KPIs
- Reporting Structure
- Daily, Weekly & Monthly Tasks
- Professional Ethics

### Practical Exercise

Explain the role of an MIS Executive in your own words.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee can clearly describe the role and responsibilities.

---

## Module 3 – Google Sheets & Excel for Prime Polymart

### Objective

Develop the spreadsheet skills required for daily MIS work.

### Topics

- Navigation
- Data Entry
- Data Cleaning
- Formulas
- Pivot Tables
- Charts
- XLOOKUP
- QUERY
- FILTER
- ARRAYFORMULA
- IMPORTRANGE

### Practical Exercise

Complete a sample sales report using provided data.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee can prepare and validate spreadsheet-based reports.

---

## Module 4 – Prime Polymart Data Structure

### Objective

Understand how company data is organized and maintained.

### Topics

- Master Data
- Sales Data
- Customer Data
- Inventory Data
- Purchase Data
- Naming Standards
- Data Relationships

### Practical Exercise

Identify the correct data source for different reporting requirements.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee understands where business information is stored.

---

## Module 5 – Daily MIS Operations

### Objective

Learn the daily workflow followed by the MIS department.

### Topics

- Daily Reports
- Data Updates
- Validation
- Dashboard Updates
- Coordination with Departments
- Issue Reporting

### Practical Exercise

Perform a simulated daily reporting process.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee can complete the daily workflow independently.

---

## Module 6 – Reports & Dashboards

### Objective

Prepare and maintain business reports and dashboards.

### Topics

- Daily Reports
- Weekly Reports
- Monthly Reports
- KPI Reports
- Dashboard Review
- Data Visualization

### Practical Exercise

Update a dashboard using sample business data.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee can interpret and maintain reports accurately.

---

## Module 7 – Data Validation & Quality

### Objective

Maintain high standards of data accuracy.

### Topics

- Validation Rules
- Error Detection
- Duplicate Records
- Missing Data
- Data Cleaning
- Quality Checks

### Practical Exercise

Find and correct errors in a sample dataset.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee consistently applies data validation techniques.

---

## Module 8 – Business Communication

### Objective

Develop professional communication skills for cross-functional collaboration.

### Topics

- Email Writing
- Internal Communication
- Reporting Issues
- Meeting Etiquette
- Documentation

### Practical Exercise

Draft a professional email reporting a data issue.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee communicates clearly and professionally.

---

## Module 9 – Advanced MIS Tasks

### Objective

Develop advanced analytical and operational skills.

### Topics

- Trend Analysis
- Business Insights
- Process Improvement
- Automation Concepts
- Root Cause Analysis

### Practical Exercise

Analyze business data and recommend improvements.

### Completion Criteria

The trainee can perform advanced MIS responsibilities.

---

## Module 10 – Practical Projects

### Objective

Apply all acquired knowledge through realistic business projects.

### Project Examples

- Prepare a Sales Dashboard
- Validate Inventory Data
- Create a Monthly MIS Report
- Analyze Business Performance

### Completion Criteria

The trainee successfully completes assigned projects.

---

## Module 11 – Final Assessment

### Assessment Includes

- Theory
- Practical Exercises
- Case Studies
- Scenario-Based Questions

### Completion Criteria

The trainee meets the required competency level.

---

## Module 12 – Certification

The AI Trainer reviews:

- Module Completion
- Practical Performance
- Assessment Results
- Competency Level

The AI then recommends one of the following:

- Ready for Live Work
- Ready with Supervision
- Additional Training Required

Final certification remains subject to company approval.

---

# 5. Estimated Learning Timeline

The recommended timeline may be adjusted based on trainee performance.

- Orientation: 1 Day
- Core Training: 5–10 Days
- Practical Exercises: 3–5 Days
- Final Assessment: 1 Day

---

# 6. Program Completion

The training program is complete when:

- All modules are completed.
- Practical exercises are successfully finished.
- Required assessments are passed.
- The trainee demonstrates the expected competency level.
