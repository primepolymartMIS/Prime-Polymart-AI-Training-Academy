# 03 - Daily Tasks

## Purpose
The purpose of this document is to train every MIS Executive to perform daily operational activities in a structured, consistent, and error-free manner.

This document defines the complete daily workflow from office opening to end-of-day reporting. It ensures that all critical systems, reports, dashboards, automations, and business processes are monitored properly to maintain uninterrupted business operations.

After completing this module, every MIS Executive should be able to:

- Follow the complete daily work routine independently.
- Monitor all business-critical systems.
- Identify and resolve common issues.
- Escalate problems appropriately.
- Complete daily reporting accurately and on time.
- Maintain data quality across all company systems.

## Learning Objectives
After completing this module, the trainee will be able to:

- Understand the daily responsibilities of an MIS Executive.
- Follow the standard office workflow.
- Monitor Google Sheets, AppSheet, dashboards, and automations.
- Verify data accuracy and report integrity.
- Detect formula and script failures.
- Perform daily reporting activities.
- Communicate effectively with management and other departments.
- Follow company SOPs during daily operations.

## Office Opening Routine
## Office Opening Routine

Every MIS Executive must begin the day by following the same sequence of activities. This ensures that all business-critical systems are operational before other departments start their work.

### Step 1 – Login & Workspace Preparation
- Start your computer and connect to the internet.
- Log in to your official Google Workspace account.
- Open Gmail and review unread emails.
- Open Google Calendar (if applicable) and check today's schedule.
- Open the company's commonly used applications:
  - Google Drive
  - Google Sheets
  - AppSheet
  - DoubleTick WhatsApp
  - Browser bookmarks

### Step 2 – Review Important Emails
Check emails for:
- Management instructions
- Customer requirements
- Sales team requests
- Purchase-related updates
- Payment follow-ups
- System or automation alerts

If any task is marked as urgent, prioritize it after informing your reporting manager if required.

### Step 3 – Open Daily Working Files

Open all business-critical files before starting work, including:

- Master Spreadsheet
- O2D Files
- D2C Files
- Purchase Files
- Credit Limit Files
- Import Files
- Insurance Files
- Sales Dashboards
- MIS Dashboards
- AppSheet
- Daily Checklist
- Weekly / Monthly Checklist
- Runo Integration Files
- DoubleTick WhatsApp

### Step 4 – Confirm System Readiness

Before beginning operational work, verify that:

- Internet is working properly.
- Google Sheets load correctly.
- AppSheet is accessible.
- Required scripts executed successfully.
- Dashboards are refreshing correctly.
- No critical formula errors are visible.
- No automation failures are reported.

If any critical issue is found, document it and inform the Senior MIS Executive or Reporting Manager immediately.
