---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the role, responsibilities, expectations, and performance standards of an MIS Executive at Prime Polymart Pvt. Ltd.

It serves as the foundation for all subsequent training modules.

---

# 2. Role Summary

An MIS Executive is responsible for collecting, validating, organizing, analyzing, and presenting business data to support informed decision-making across the organization.

The role requires accuracy, analytical thinking, attention to detail, and the ability to transform raw data into meaningful reports and dashboards.

---

# 3. Mission of the Role

The mission of an MIS Executive is to provide accurate, timely, and reliable business information that enables departments and management to make better operational and strategic decisions.

---

Supports:

- Daily Operations

- Performance Reporting
- Decision Making

---

# 5. Primary Responsibilities

An MIS Executive is responsible for:

- Collecting business data from approved sources.
- Validating data accuracy and completeness.
- Maintaining company spreadsheets and databases.
- Preparing daily, weekly, and monthly reports.
- Updating dashboards.
- Monitoring business KPIs.
- Identifying inconsistencies and reporting issues.
- Supporting different departments with required information.
- Maintaining proper documentation.
- Following company SOPs and reporting standards.

# 8. Daily Responsibilities

Typical daily responsibilities include:

- Reviewing assigned tasks.
- Updating business data.
- Validating new entries.
- Preparing scheduled reports.
- Updating dashboards.
- Responding to internal data requests.
- Reporting discrepancies.
- Coordinating with relevant departments.

---

# 9. Weekly Responsibilities

Examples include:

- Department coordination.

# 10. Monthly Responsibilities

Examples include:

- Monthly MIS reports.
- KPI analysis.
- Performance summaries.
- Data reconciliation.
- Report archiving.
- Management presentations.

---

# 11. Common Challenges

An MIS Executive may encounter:

- Incomplete data.
- Duplicate records.
- Incorrect formulas.
- Missing reports.
- Delayed updates.
- Communication gaps.
- Data inconsistencies.

The AI Trainer should prepare trainees to identify and resolve these issues using approved company procedures.

---

# 12. Best Practices

The AI should encourage trainees to:

- Verify data before reporting.
- Follow SOPs consistently.
- Maintain organized files.
- Document important changes.
- Communicate issues promptly.
- Protect confidential information.
- Review work before submission.

---

# 13. Learning Outcomes

After completing this module, trainees should be able to:

- Explain the role of an MIS Executive.
- Describe key responsibilities.
- Identify required skills.
- Understand reporting expectations.
- Recognize performance standards.
- Explain how the role supports business operations.
