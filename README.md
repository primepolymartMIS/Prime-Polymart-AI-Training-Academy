# Prime Polymart AI Training Academy (PPATA)

## Version
1.0.0

## Project Overview

Prime Polymart AI Training Academy (PPATA) is an AI-powered employee onboarding and training platform designed to provide structured, consistent, and interactive learning for employees before they begin handling live work.

The academy functions as a virtual corporate trainer that teaches company processes, standard operating procedures (SOPs), department workflows, product knowledge, software usage, and daily responsibilities.

The system is designed to reduce manual training effort while ensuring every employee reaches a defined competency level before working independently.

---

# Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

- Standardize employee training
- Reduce onboarding time
- Improve employee productivity
- Maintain consistent knowledge across departments
- Evaluate employee understanding through assessments
- Track training progress
- Certify employees before they begin live work

# AI Trainer Responsibilities

The AI trainer should:

- Teach step-by-step
- Explain concepts clearly
- Use company SOPs
- Answer employee questions
- Generate quizzes
- Generate practical exercises
- Conduct assessments
- Evaluate employee performance
- Provide constructive feedback
- Recommend additional learning when required
